How to make a grilled cheese
1. Get your ingredients

You are going to need

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese
2. Prep

Heat up your pan while you put the cheese between two slices of bread.

3. Start cooking
  1. Once the pan is hot melt a small piece of butter in the pan.
  2. Place your uncooked sandwhich in the pan and move it around the pan to soak up the butter.
  3. Place a lid over the pan to keep the heat inside of the pan and to melt the cheese.
  4. Periodically check the sandwhich to make sure it isn't burning.
  5. Once the first side of the sandwhich is cooked to your liking, remove the sandwhich, repeat step 3 for the other side, then move on to step 4.
4. Eat

You are done making your grilled cheese! Cut your sandwhich if desired and enjoy!
